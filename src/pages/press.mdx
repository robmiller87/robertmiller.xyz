## The Through-Line

**2017:** Participated in crypto's first decentralized ICO (dICO) with Monaize — trustless, atomic-swap-based fundraising that eliminated centralized escrow.

**2021-2023:** VP of Growth at Fuse Network — building payments infrastructure for real-world crypto adoption.

**2024+:** Building physical community infrastructure for the Startup Nation movement. Advising founders on positioning, capital, and the relationships that matter.

*Same thesis evolving: making financial sovereignty accessible to ordinary people.*
